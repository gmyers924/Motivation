Motivational Figures

Motivational figures could help a person become much more motivated. It could help you see that there are great people out there and that they could influence people to do things. It could be relaxing that they are not alone and have someone to relate to.
Some inspirational people in the past include:

  • Martin Luther King Jr. – gave moving speeches to change things in history and was a huge part of the equal rights movement.
  • Morgan Freeman – Actor who is known for his wise words and for caring for others.
  • Sir Isaac Newton – discovered gravity and was the first person to calculate the speed of sound.
  • William Shakespeare - Writer and poet who changed the English world with a great play like Romeo & Juliet.
  • Adam Sandler – Actor who is known for making lots of motivating comedy movies with his studio called Happy Maddison.
  • Rosa Parks – First lady of civil rights.
  • Amelia Earhart - First female to fly across the Atlantic Ocean alone.
With motivation, there are endless possibilities.
